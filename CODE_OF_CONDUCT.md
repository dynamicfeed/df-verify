# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this to be an open, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless of background or experience. We pledge to act in ways that keep the community welcoming, respectful, and focused on good technical work.

## Standards

Examples of behaviour that helps:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the people in it.

Examples of behaviour that is not acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption, spam, or coordinated inauthentic activity.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove or reject contributions, comments, and contributors that violate this code. To report an issue, contact **hello@dynamicfeed.ai**. Reports will be handled discreetly.

This Code of Conduct adapts the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
